About Us

OHITS is an acronym which means “Out here in these Streets”. It is a production company for the Arts/Entertainment Industry. It was created by a house of Creatives with the sole purpose to produce positive content that will inspire as well as entertain. Whether, that medium is Film, music, theatre, apparel, tech, books, or digital art our main objective is to collectively build bridges of innovation/creation to community. Building upon these various platforms will allow us to make connections that will ultimately change lives. “We believe that Creators that create together, will Inspire together!”

Rod G. Newman

Owner of OHITS Production House, Conceptual Performance Artist- My passion is to inspire the minds of men and women in the local communities to Create the Change.

How OHITS was Founded

Founded by Conceptual performance artist, Rod G. Newman and Actor/Co-Founder Dean Anthony - two artists with one goal: CREATE. Our mission together will create the movement needed to get our thoughts and ideas out of our head, and in the Streets. With this company, we have a designed an atmosphere and culture to freely produce these ideas. Although, “the house is our home, the Streets are waiting...”

- Rod G. Newman
Dean Anthony
